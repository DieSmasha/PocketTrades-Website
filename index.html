Frequently asked questions.

What trades is PocketTrades built for? +

Plumbers, electricians, HVAC techs, general contractors, carpenters, painters, landscapers, roofers, welders, drywallers, tile setters, flooring installers, insulation contractors, and more. The app pre-loads hundreds of common catalogue items specific to your trade so you can invoice faster from day one.

Does it really work offline? +

Yes. Every feature works without internet. Your data is stored locally on your device first. When cloud sync launches, it will sync in the background when you have a connection.

Is it only for Canadians? +

PocketTrades is designed for Canadian tradespeople with built-in GST/HST/PST/QST handling and CAD currency. We plan to expand to other markets in the future.

Is my data safe? +

Your data lives on your device. We don't sell your information, and our privacy policy is written in plain language. When cloud sync launches, your data will be encrypted and stored securely.

Can I try it before paying? +

Absolutely. The free tier lets you create up to 5 invoices and 5 quotes per month with no time limit. Upgrade to Pro only when your business needs it.

What if I get a new phone? +

Use the Google Drive backup in Settings to back up all your data, then restore it on your new device in seconds. Everything comes back — customers, invoices, jobs, quotes, your business logo, the whole thing.

What platforms are supported? +

Android and iOS. Desktop support (Windows, Linux, macOS) is on our roadmap for v2.5.
